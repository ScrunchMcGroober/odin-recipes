Pizza

Description

Pizza is a cylindrical food item typically containing ingredients. Any number of objects can be placed on the cylinder to yield pizza, but common ones include curdled bovine milk, nature pustules, intestinal blockages, and liberty bells.

Ingredients

  • Dough Cylinder
  • Hard Milk
  • Earthen Blisters
  • Oven
  • Child-Bearing Rocks

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to hot
  2. Place Dough Cylinder into Oven for time
  3. After time, remove Dough Cylinder from Oven
  4. Place cold Curdled Milk on Dough Cylinder
  5. Slather with Earthern Blister pureé
  6. Break several Child-Bearing Rocks on it.
